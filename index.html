About Me

What I Do Now

For most of my career, I've made a living from being the resident computer geek on a team of business associates. Most recently, my role for the last seven years has been primarily based in a somewhat niche subset of front-end, working in SharePoint and Power Platform development. I design and maintain site collections, intake forms, and automation workflows to make the lives of my colleagues just a bit easier.

Ready for a Change

I had already started learning more general front-end development months ago, but recently my current employer announced a change in their business model. While good for the business as a whole, it would mean that over the next several months, many positions would no longer be needed, including those of me and my colleagues. So this seemed like the perfect time to begin exploring opportunities to take the next step towards becoming a full-time developer.

Character Concept Generator

As a long-time tabletop nerd, I've taught many people to play games like D&D or Pathfinder over the years. One of the most common obstacles new players have is being overwhelmed with options and not knowing what they want to play. This tool, which generates a random character concept was a project I began to envision from my very first lesson in the Vue.js framework. I've already shared it with some of my gaming friends, and some of them already have players they want to show it to before their next games.
